Who We Are

Big Sister Cooking was created to feel like being in the kitchen with someone who cares about you — a big sister who's patient, encouraging, and always willing to help. Cooking can feel overwhelming at first, especially when you're young or just starting out, and this site exists to make that process feel warm, safe, and enjoyable. Here, recipes are shared with love, guidance, and the belief that anyone can learn to cook something great. If there's a meal that means something to you or a dish you've always wanted to learn, we'd be more than happy to teach you how to make it, step by step, until it feels like your own.

Contact Us

Whether you're just starting out or looking to improve your skills, Big Sister Cooking is here to help. Reach out with questions, feedback, or recipe requests.
